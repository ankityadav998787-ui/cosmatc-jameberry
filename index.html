🔄 Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Return Policy

Eligibility

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. To be eligible, items must be unused, in original condition, and in original packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened skincare or beauty products (for hygiene reasons)
  • Gift cards or promotional credits
  • Items marked "Final Sale"

How to Initiate a Return

Contact us at support@glowbeautystore.com or call (606) 980-0000 with your order number. We will provide a prepaid return label for defective items.

Refund Timeline

Once your return is received and inspected (1–3 business days), we will notify you via email. Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Exchanges

We replace items that are defective or damaged. Contact us and we'll ship a replacement at no cost.

Cancellations

Orders may be cancelled within 24 hours of placement. Once shipped, the return process applies.
